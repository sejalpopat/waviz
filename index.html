WikiArt Emotions DataSet is a dataset of 4,105 pieces of art that are annotated with the emotions they evoked in observers. The pieces of art were selected from WikiArt.org's collection for twenty-two categories from four western styles (Renaissance Art, Post-Renaissance Art, Modern Art, and Contemporary Art). The art is annotated via crowdsourcing for one or more of twenty emotion categories. We invite you to explore the insights from the data below.

Art is highly subjective, often eliciting different reactions and interpretations depending on the viewer. In this case, all works of art were evaluated for a wide range of emotions. Below, we offer a deeper look into two emotions that run in direct contrast one another: happiness and sadness.
